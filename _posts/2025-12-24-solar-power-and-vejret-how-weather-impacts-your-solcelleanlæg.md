## How Vejret Affects Your Solar Investment

### Sun, Clouds, and Everything In Between

It’s true: solceller work best when the sun is shining. However, modern solcelleanlæg are surprisingly efficient even on cloudy days. Here’s why:

- **Diffuse Light:** Even when it’s overcast, solar panels absorb diffuse sunlight. You might not get peak output, but your system will still generate power.
- **Cool Climates:** Solceller actually perform better in cooler temperatures. In countries like Denmark, Norway, and Sweden, your panels are less likely to overheat, keeping them efficient through multiple seasons.
- **Long Summer Days:** Nordic summers are famous for their long daylight hours—perfect for maximizing your solar yield.

### Weatherproof and Reliable

Worried about snow, rain, or wind? No problem. Today’s bedste solcelleanlæg are designed to withstand harsh weather. Panels are tested for heavy snow loads and strong winds, and most are self-cleaning when it rains.
