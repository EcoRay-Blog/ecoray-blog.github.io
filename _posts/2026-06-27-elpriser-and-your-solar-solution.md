## Why Do Elpriser Fluctuate?

Elpriser are influenced by a mix of factors, from energy supply and demand to global events and government policies. In Northern Europe and the USA, unpredictable weather, resource availability, and market dynamics all play a role in how much you pay for electricity each month. These ups and downs make it difficult to predict your bills—and even harder to plan your household or business budget.
