## How Weather Impacts Your Solar Panels in Denmark, Sweden, Norway, and the USA

Many people wonder if solar panels can thrive in regions with variable weather. The good news? Modern solceller are designed for efficiency in a wide range of climates, from sunny California and Texas to the cloudier skies of Denmark, Sweden, and Norway. Here’s how different weather conditions can influence your solcelleanlæg:

- **Sunshine:** More sun generally means higher electricity generation, helping you spar på elregning (save on your electricity bill).
- **Clouds:** Even on overcast days, solar panels capture diffuse sunlight. While output drops slightly, your system keeps producing clean energy.
- **Rain and Snow:** Rain helps wash away dust, keeping your solceller working efficiently. Snow usually slides off angled panels, but a temporary cover won’t damage the system.
- **Cold Weather:** Surprisingly, cooler temperatures can boost panel efficiency. Solar cells work best when they’re not overheated!
