## Why Do Strømafbrydelser Happen?

Strømafbrydelser can occur for many reasons: severe weather, grid failures, planned maintenance, or unexpected technical issues. In Scandinavia and the USA, energy grids are generally reliable, but even short interruptions can cause inconvenience or financial loss. That’s why more and more homeowners and businesses are looking into **solceller** (solar panels) and **solcelleanlæg** (solar panel systems) as a proactive solution.

- **Solceller til hus** (solar panels for homes) generate electricity from the sun, reducing reliance on the grid.
- **Solceller med batteri** (solar with battery) store excess energy, so you have backup power when you need it most.
