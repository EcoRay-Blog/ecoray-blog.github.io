## What Is Nettopower and Why Does It Matter?

Nettopower refers to the concept of using the electricity you produce with your own solceller, and then sending any surplus energy back to the grid. This “net metering” approach means you only pay for the electricity you actually use from your energy provider, while earning credits (or even a payout) for what you contribute. It’s a win-win: save on your elregning (electricity bill), lower your carbon footprint, and make your home or business more resilient.

By choosing the best solcelleanlæg, you maximize your savings and get the most out of nettopower.
