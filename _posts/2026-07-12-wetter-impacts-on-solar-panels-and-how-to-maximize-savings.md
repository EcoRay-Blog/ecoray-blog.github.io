## How Wetter Conditions Affect Solceller Performance

It’s no secret that solceller work best in direct sunlight. However, many are surprised to learn that modern solceller til hus and businesses produce energy even on cloudy or rainy days. Here’s how:

- **Clouds and Rain:** While heavy clouds reduce the amount of sunlight reaching your solcelleanlæg, panels still generate power from diffused light. In fact, cooler, wetter conditions can help panels operate more efficiently, since extreme heat sometimes reduces output.
- **Self-Cleaning Effect:** Regular rainfall helps keep your panels free from dust and debris, maintaining high efficiency without frequent manual cleaning.
- **Seasonal Changes:** Shorter days and lower sun angles in Nordic winters do reduce total output, but summer’s long daylight hours help balance your annual production.
