## Vejret and Solar: What You Need to Know

It’s easy to think that only sunny regions can benefit from solceller, but that’s a myth! Modern solcelleanlæg are designed to work efficiently in a variety of weather conditions. Here’s how vejret influences your system and what it means for you:

- **Cloudy Days:** Even on overcast days, your solceller generate electricity. While direct sunlight boosts performance, diffused light still powers your home or business.
- **Snow and Cold:** In Scandinavia and northern USA, snow can reflect sunlight, sometimes increasing output on clear days. Plus, solceller actually work more efficiently in cooler temperatures.
- **Long Summer Days:** The extended daylight in Nordic summers means more hours for your solcelleanlæg to generate electricity, helping you save big on your elregning.
