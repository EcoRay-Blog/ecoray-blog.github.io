## Weather and Solar Power: What You Need to Know

Contrary to popular belief, solceller don’t need uninterrupted sunshine to work their magic. Modern solcelleanlæg are designed to capture energy even on cloudy days. Here’s how different weather conditions interact with solar technology:

- **Sunny Days:** The best friend of any solar system. Clear skies mean maximum energy production—perfect for those looking to spar på elregning and maximize their investment.
- **Cloudy or Overcast:** Solceller still generate electricity, just at a reduced rate. In Northern Europe and parts of the USA, quality panels and AI-based system recommendations ensure you get the most out of every available daylight hour.
- **Rain and Snow:** While heavy snow can temporarily block sunlight, most solcelleanlæg are installed at angles that help snow slide off. Rain actually helps by washing away dust and debris, keeping your panels efficient.
