## Why Solceller Are a Smart Response to Stroemafbrudelse

Traditional electricity grids are vulnerable to weather, equipment failures, and rising energy demands. When a stroemafbrudelse strikes, homes and businesses can be left in the dark—sometimes for hours. By investing in a modern solcelleanlæg (solar panel system), you can minimize your risk of power interruptions and ensure you always have access to the energy you need.

**Solceller til hus** (solar panels for homes) and **solceller med batteri** (solar panels with battery storage) are especially effective solutions for power security. Here’s why:

- **Energy Independence**: Generate your own electricity, reducing reliance on the grid.
- **Backup Power**: Store excess solar energy in batteries, so you’re covered during a stroemafbrudelse.
