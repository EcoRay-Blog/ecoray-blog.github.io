## Does Weather Affect Solceller Performance?

Absolutely—but maybe not in the way you think! Here’s what actually happens:

- **Cloudy Days:** Even in Denmark, Sweden, or Norway, where clouds are common, modern solceller still generate electricity. While output drops a bit, panels capture diffuse sunlight through clouds, keeping your home powered and your elregning lower.
- **Rainy Weather:** Rain helps clean your solceller, washing away dust and pollen so they work more efficiently. Plus, cooler temperatures can actually increase panel efficiency.
- **Snowy Conditions:** In Scandinavia and the northern US, snow might cover panels for a few days. But since panels are installed at an angle, snow usually slides off quickly. Reflective snow can even boost performance by bouncing extra sunlight onto your panels.
- **Hot Summers:** Surprisingly, extreme heat can slightly reduce panel efficiency. But with the right solcelleanlæg and smart AI-based recommendations from EcoRay, your system stays optimized year-round.
