## How Temperature Influences Solar Panel Performance

It’s a common myth that hotter weather always means more solar energy. While **solceller** need sunlight, high temperatures can actually reduce their efficiency. Here’s what happens:

- **Solar panels work best at moderate temperatures**. When it gets too hot, the panels’ electrical components can lose efficiency. This doesn’t mean Nordic countries are at a disadvantage—cool, sunny days are ideal!
- **Cold, sunny climates are perfect for solceller**. If you live in Scandinavia or northern USA, your panels might perform better than you think.
- **System quality matters**. The **bedste solcelleanlæg** are designed to minimize temperature losses, so you get reliable performance year-round.
