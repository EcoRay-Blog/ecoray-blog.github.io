## How Vejret Shapes Solar Potential

Vejret affects how much sunlight reaches your solceller, but the impact is often less dramatic than you might think. Even in areas with frequent clouds or snow, today’s best solar panels are engineered to perform efficiently. Here’s how:

- **Cloudy Days:** Solceller still generate power, even when skies are grey. Diffused sunlight can be captured and converted into electricity, just at a slightly reduced rate.
- **Winter Conditions:** Snow can temporarily cover panels, but in Scandinavia and North America, panels are designed for easy snow-shedding and work well in cold temperatures. In fact, solar cells are more efficient in cooler weather!
- **Long Summer Days:** In northern regions, those extended daylight hours from May to August mean your solcelleanlæg can produce a surplus, especially when paired with solceller med batteri for energy storage.
