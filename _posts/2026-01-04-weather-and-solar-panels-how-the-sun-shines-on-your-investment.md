## How Weather Influences Solar Panel Performance in Denmark, Sweden, Norway, and the USA

Let’s break it down simply. Solceller work by converting sunlight into electricity. Naturally, more sun means more power. But it’s not all about endless sunshine:

- **Cloudy Days:** Even on overcast days in Denmark, Sweden, or Norway, your solceller continue to generate electricity—just at a slightly reduced rate. Modern solcelleanlæg are designed to capture diffuse sunlight, so you’re still saving on your elregning.
- **Cold Weather:** Contrary to what many think, solceller are actually more efficient in cooler temperatures. So those crisp Scandinavian winters can be a plus!
- **Rain and Snow:** While heavy snow can temporarily cover panels, most installations are tilted so snow slides off. Rain actually helps by keeping panels clean and performing at their best.
- **Sunshine in the USA:** In states like California or Texas, long sunny days can supercharge your solcelle investering and accelerate your return.
