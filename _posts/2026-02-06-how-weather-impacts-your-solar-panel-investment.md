## Sun, Snow, and Savings: Solceller in Every Climate

You might be surprised to learn that solceller til hus (solar panels for homes) are designed to perform in a variety of weather conditions—not just endless sunshine.

- **Cloudy Days:** Even under clouds, modern solceller generate electricity. While output is lower, you’ll still see savings on your elregning (electricity bill).
- **Snowy Winters:** In Scandinavia and parts of the USA, snow can reflect extra sunlight onto your panels, giving them a boost once the snow slides off. Plus, cold temperatures actually help solceller run more efficiently.
- **Rainy Seasons:** Rain helps wash away dust and debris, keeping your solcelleanlæg performing at its best.
