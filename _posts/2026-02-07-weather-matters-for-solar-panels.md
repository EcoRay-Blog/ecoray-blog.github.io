## How Weather Impacts Your Solar Cell System

Weather plays a key role in the performance of a **solcelleanlæg**. Let’s break down what private homeowners and businesses across Denmark, Sweden, Norway, and the USA need to know:

- **Sunlight Is Key**  
  Solar panels generate the most energy on bright, sunny days. Even in the Nordics, the long summer days offer plenty of power for your **solceller til hus** or commercial setup.
- **Cloudy Days Still Count**  
  Modern **solceller** are designed to work efficiently even on cloudy or overcast days. While output may dip, your system will continue to generate electricity and help you **spar på elregning**.
- **Snow and Cold Weather**  
  Snow can actually help by cleaning your panels as it slides off, and cold temperatures make solar panels work more efficiently. That makes **solceller Danmark**, Sweden, and Norway a smart choice, even in winter.
- **Heat and Solar Efficiency**  
  Believe it or not, extreme heat can reduce the efficiency of solar panels. Systems in the USA benefit from careful placement and design to maximize output year-round.
