## Why Solar Power Matters in Greenland

Traditionally, Greenland relies heavily on imported fossil fuels for electricity and heating. But with advances in technology and growing awareness of climate change, the demand for clean energy solutions is rising. Solar panels, or *solceller*, are becoming increasingly attractive for both residential homes (*solceller til hus*) and commercial buildings.

Here’s why more Greenlanders, and forward-thinking neighbors across Scandinavia and North America, are turning to solar:

- **Abundant seasonal sunlight**: Despite long winter nights, Greenland enjoys almost endless daylight in summer—perfect for harvesting solar energy.
- **Lower energy costs**: Curious about *hvad koster solceller*? With the right *solcelleanlæg*, you can dramatically *spar på elregning* (save on your electric bill).
- **Sustainability**: Solar power reduces dependence on imported fuels and supports local, renewable energy production.
