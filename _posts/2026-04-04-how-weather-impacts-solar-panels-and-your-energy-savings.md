## Weather and Solar Panels Explained

It’s a common myth that solceller only work well in regions with endless sunshine. In fact, today’s intelligent solar systems are designed to perform efficiently in a variety of weather conditions—including cloudy and cool climates typical of Scandinavia and parts of America.

**Here’s how different weather conditions influence your solar panels:**

- **Sunny Days:** Maximum energy production. This is when your solceller til hus or business will generate the most electricity and help you spar på elregning.
- **Cloudy or Overcast:** Modern solcelleanlæg still produce power on cloudy days, just at a slightly reduced rate. Thanks to AI-based system recommendations, EcoRay helps you choose the best solar setup for your region’s weather patterns.
- **Rain and Snow:** Light rain can actually help clean your panels, improving their efficiency. Snow can temporarily reduce output, but most solceller are angled so snow slides off or melts quickly.
- **Cold Weather:** Contrary to popular belief, solceller often work even more efficiently in cool temperatures, since heat can slightly lower performance.
