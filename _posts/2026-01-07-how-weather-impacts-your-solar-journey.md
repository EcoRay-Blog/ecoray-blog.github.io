## Why Weather Matters for Solceller

Solar panels work by converting sunlight into electricity. It’s easy to assume that only hot, sunny climates are ideal for solceller til hus, but the reality is much brighter for northern homeowners and businesses:

- **Efficiency in Cool Climates:** Solceller actually operate more efficiently in cooler temperatures. In regions like Denmark, Sweden, and Norway, long summer days and cooler air combine for impressive energy yields.
- **Cloudy Days Still Count:** Even on overcast days, your solcelleanlæg generates electricity. Modern solar technology captures diffuse sunlight, so you’re not left in the dark.
- **Seasonal Variations:** While winter brings shorter days, snow can reflect sunlight onto panels, boosting production. Plus, solceller med batteri can store excess summer energy for use in darker months.
