Around Denmark, Sweden, Norway, and the USA, solar energy is picking up speed. The reasons are clear:

- **Lower energy bills:** With solceller til hus (solar panels for homes), you can spar på elregning (save on your electricity bill) immediately.
- **Attractive returns:** Solcelle investering (solar investment) is increasingly popular thanks to stable prices and long-term savings.
- **Sustainability:** Solar power is a clean, renewable resource that reduces your carbon footprint.
- **Government incentives:** Many regions offer solceller og tilskud (solar subsidies) to make køb solceller (buying solar panels) more affordable.
