## How Weather Influences Solar Panel Performance

Solar panels (solceller) work by capturing sunlight and converting it into electricity. It's true that more sun means higher output, but the relationship isn't always straightforward. Here’s what you need to know:

- **Cloudy Days:** Modern solcelleanlæg are efficient even on overcast days, producing 10-25% of their rated capacity.
- **Rain:** While heavy rain reduces output temporarily, it helps keep your panels clean, which supports better performance over time.
- **Snow:** In regions like Denmark, Sweden, and Norway, snow can cover panels, but most systems are designed at an angle so snow slides off. Plus, the bright white surface can actually reflect sunlight and boost efficiency after it's cleared.
- **Cold Weather:** Surprisingly, solceller often work more efficiently in cooler temperatures.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

### 1. Hvordan påvirker vejret effektiviteten af solceller til hus i Danmark?  
Vejret påvirker effektiviteten, men moderne solcelleanlæg fungerer også på overskyede dage og i koldt klima. Solceller kan producere mellem 10-25% af deres kapacitet på overskyede dage, og koldere temperaturer kan endda øge effektiviteten.
