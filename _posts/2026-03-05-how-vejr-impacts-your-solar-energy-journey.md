## Why Vejr Matters for Solceller

Vejr is more than just sunshine. It includes cloud cover, temperature, rainfall, snow, and even wind. Each aspect influences how much energy your solceller can generate and how quickly you see savings on your electricity bill.

- **Sunny Days:** Maximize output and savings. Even in the Nordics, long summer days can deliver impressive returns on your solcelle investering.
- **Cloudy Weather:** Modern solcelleanlæg are more efficient than ever and can convert diffuse light into electricity, so you still benefit even when the sky is overcast.
- **Snow & Rain:** While snow can temporarily cover panels, its reflective quality can also boost solar production on clear days. Rain keeps your panels clean, which helps maintain efficiency.
