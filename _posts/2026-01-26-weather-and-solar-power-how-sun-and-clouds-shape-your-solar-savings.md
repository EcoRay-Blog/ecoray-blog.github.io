### Sunny Days Drive Savings

It’s simple—more sunlight means more energy. On clear days, your solceller til hus work at peak performance, generating electricity that can drastically reduce your electric bill. Many homeowners are surprised by just how much they can save on their elregning, even in regions where sunshine isn’t always guaranteed.

### What About Cloudy or Snowy Weather?

Cloudy skies, rain, and even snow are part of daily life in Scandinavia and much of the USA. But modern solcelleanlæg are designed to perform in all kinds of weather:

- **Cloudy Days:** Even when the sun hides, your solar panels still capture diffused sunlight. While output drops, you’re still generating power.
- **Snow:** A light dusting of snow can actually improve efficiency by reflecting more sunlight onto your panels. Heavier snow may temporarily block sunlight, but most panels are installed at an angle, allowing snow to slide off quickly.
- **Cold Temperatures:** Contrary to popular belief, cold weather can improve the efficiency of solceller. Solar panels operate more efficiently in cool temperatures as long as the sun is shining.
