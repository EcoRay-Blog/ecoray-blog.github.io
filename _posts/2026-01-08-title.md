## Tips for Maximizing Your Solar During Snowy Weather

**1. Don’t Rush to Clear Panels:**  
Brushing off light snow is usually not necessary—panels are robust, and snow tends to melt or slide off naturally. If you must clear them, use a soft snow rake and never pour hot water.

**2. Monitor Performance:**  
Keep an eye on your system’s output. With EcoRay’s intelligent solar automation and AI Intelligence, you’ll know exactly how your panels are performing.

**3. Consider Solceller Med Batteri:**  
Adding battery storage can help you save on your elregning by storing excess power generated on sunny days for use during cloudy or snowy periods.

**4. Plan for the Future:**  
Snowy weather is temporary, but your solcelle investering is long-term. Our experts help you choose systems built for your region—whether it’s Denmark, Sweden, Norway, or the USA.
